Wireframes

Wireframes provide a foundational view of an interface’s structure, functionality, and intended user behavior. This process omits extensive color, style, and graphics, encouraging feedback and iterative changes.

By developing wireframes for both websites and mobile applications, I’ve learned to distinguish guidelines for space allocation, functionality, and user expectations according to device format.

Mid-fidelity wireframes that include interface structure, functionality, typography, icons, and shading; portray an e-commerce mobile app interface.

High fidelity wireframes that apply color and other branding elements.
