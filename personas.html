Personas

User personas are fictional archetypes derived from user research. Each represents the needs, goals, and other attributes of a particular group of users. Personas put a human face to users and enable the team to consider how feature and design decisions might support or hinder those users in achieving their goals.

Whether portrayed in the form of an actual persona or listed elsewhere, my projects have focused on identifying the primary users of the product or service. Understanding and advocating for a target audience separates UX Design from other disciplines.
