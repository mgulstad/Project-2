Style Guides

Given the iterative nature of digital interfaces, developing a set of rules and guidelines to direct design choices and other elements assures a consistent and cohesive product. Style guides not only provide designers a place to organize and display design choices, they also include beneficial CSS attributes that allow developers to quickly implement the established styles.

While my style guides haven’t been based on official branding guidelines or client requests, I’ve created pseudo-branding to practice applying consistent design choices throughout an interface prototype.
