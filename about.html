About me

I’m a full-time student at the University of Minnesota; majoring in Technical Writing and Communication (TWC) and minoring in UX Design, I’m building a diverse skillset to adapt to the expansive field of user experience design. By learning how to properly structure and explain complex information in a user-friendly manner, I’m approaching the field with a more unique perspective.

By supplementing my knowledge of information architecture, collaborative communication, and document design with design principles and experience producing multimedia deliverables, I strive to balance the creative and analytical aspects necessary to both design effective digital interfaces and advocate for its intended users.

In my free time, I enjoy watching baseball and pursuing a number of outdoor activities during the warmer seasons, and enjoy curling up with a good book or show and alpine skiing during the colder seasons. Overall, I love to learn and connect with people, fostering a better understanding of my surroundings and considering how I can contribute to the discourses and communities I’m a part of.
