Prototypes

Like wireframes, low- to high-fidelity prototypes offer different degrees of feedback about a user’s experience with a product. Low-fidelity prototypes prioritize structuring the basic navigation and user-flow of an interface. High-fidelity prototypes focus on refining the user’s experience following the application of more specific design and branding UI elements.

While I haven’t explicitly developed low-fidelity prototypes, I’ve progressed through the range of prototypes in order to sharpen my understanding of a user’s journey throughout a digital interface.
